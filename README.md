## About

Tervia Corporation™ is an independent digital company founded in December 2025.  
Built with long-term vision and structural clarity, Tervia operates with a focused and scalable approach to interactive software and original intellectual property development.

The company is currently composed of two core members and operates with a lean, high-control development model.

---

## Structure

Tervia currently operates through two official divisions:

### Tervia Corporation™
Corporate structure, brand ownership, intellectual property management, and strategic direction.

### Tervia Interactive™
Game development division focused on interactive digital experiences and original projects.
